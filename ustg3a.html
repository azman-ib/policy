Value Added Tax Act (UStG) § 3a Place of supply of other services (1) Subject to paragraphs 2 to 8 and sections 3b and 3e, other services are deemed to be performed at the place from which the supplier carries on business. If the other service is performed from a permanent establishment, that permanent establishment is deemed to be the place of performance. (2) Any other service supplied to a business for the purposes of that business shall, subject to paragraphs 3 to 8 and sections 3b and 3e, be supplied at the place from which the recipient carries on business. If the other service is supplied to a business's permanent establishment, the place of that permanent establishment shall be the determining factor. Sentences 1 and 2 shall apply mutatis mutandis to any other service supplied to a legal person who is exclusively engaged in non-business activities and who has been issued a VAT identification number, and to any other service supplied to a legal person who is engaged in both business and non-business activities; this shall not apply to other services that are intended exclusively for the private use of staff or a shareholder. (3) By way of derogation from paragraphs 1 and 2: 1. Other services related to real estate are performed where the real estate is located. These include, in particular: a) other services of the type referred to in Section 4 No. 12, b) other services related to the sale or purchase of real estate, c) other services that serve the development of land or the preparation, coordination or execution of construction work. 2. The short-term rental of a means of transport is deemed to take place at the location where the means of transport is actually made available to the recipient. For the purposes of the first sentence, short-term rental is defined as a rental for an uninterrupted period. a) of no more than 90 days for watercraft, b) of no more than 30 days for other means of transport. The rental of a means of transport, which is not considered short-term within the meaning of sentence 2, to a recipient who is neither a business for whose business the service is procured nor a non-business legal entity to which a VAT identification number has been issued, is deemed to be supplied at the place where the recipient has their residence or registered office. If the means of transport is a recreational craft, then, notwithstanding sentence 3, the rental service is deemed to be supplied at the place where the recreational craft is actually made available to the recipient, provided that the registered office, management, or a permanent establishment of the supplier from which this service is actually supplied is also located at that place. 3. Cultural, artistic, scientific, educational, sporting, entertainment, or similar services, such as services related to trade fairs and exhibitions, including the services of the respective organizers and related activities essential for the performance of these services, are supplied to a recipient who is neither a business for whose business the service is performed nor a non-business legal person who has been issued a VAT identification number, where the services are actually provided by the supplier. If the services are transmitted via streaming or made available virtually in any other way, the place of supply, notwithstanding the first sentence, is the place where the recipient is established, has their residence, or is habitually resident. 3a. The following services will be performed where they are actually carried out by the contractor: a) The provision of food and beverages for consumption on the premises (restaurant services), when such provision does not take place on board a ship, aircraft or railway during transport within the territory of the Community; b) Work on movable tangible objects and the assessment of these objects for a recipient who is neither an entrepreneur for whose business the service is performed, nor a non-entrepreneurial legal person who has been issued a VAT identification number. 4. A brokerage service provided to a recipient who is neither an entrepreneur for whose business the service is procured, nor a non-entrepreneurial legal person who has been issued a VAT identification number, is deemed to be provided at the place where the brokered transaction is deemed to have taken place. 5. The granting of admission rights to cultural, artistic, scientific, educational, sporting, entertainment, or similar events, such as trade fairs and exhibitions, as well as related services, to a business for its business purposes or to a non-business legal entity that has been issued a VAT identification number, is deemed to be performed at the place where the event is actually held. If the admission right enables virtual participation in an event, paragraph 2 applies to the granting of this admission right, notwithstanding the first sentence. (4) If the recipient of one of the other services referred to in sentence 2 is neither a business for whose business the service is procured nor a non-business legal person to whom a VAT identification number has been issued, and is resident or headquartered in a third country, the other service shall be deemed to be performed at his or her place of residence or headquarter. Other services within the meaning of sentence 1 are: 1. the granting, transfer and exercise of patents, copyrights, trademark rights and similar rights; 2. other services that serve advertising or public relations, including the services of advertising intermediaries and advertising agencies; 3. other services arising from activities as a lawyer, patent attorney, tax advisor, tax agent, auditor, certified public accountant, expert, engineer, supervisory board member, interpreter and translator, as well as similar services of other entrepreneurs, in particular legal, economic and technical advice; 4. data processing; 5. the provision of information, including commercial processes and experience; 6. a) Banking and financial transactions, in particular those of the type referred to in Section 4, number 8, letters a to h, and the administration of loans and loan collateral, as well as insurance transactions of the type referred to in Section 4, number 10, b) Other services related to the trade in gold, silver, and platinum. (5) If the recipient is one of the other benefits referred to in sentence 2 1. no entrepreneur for whose business the service is being obtained, 2. no exclusively non-business legal entity that has been issued a VAT identification number, 3. no legal entity that is both commercially and non-commercially active, where the service is not intended exclusively for the private needs of the staff or a shareholder, The other service is performed at the place where the recipient of the service has their residence, habitual abode, or registered office. Other services within the meaning of sentence 1 are: 1. other services in the field of telecommunications; 2. the broadcasting and television services; 3. other services provided electronically. Paragraph 1 does not apply if the supplier has its registered office, place of management, a permanent establishment, or, in the absence of a registered office, place of management, or permanent establishment, its residence or habitual abode in only one Member State, and the total amount of consideration for other services referred to in paragraph 2 to recipients referred to in paragraph 1 with residence, habitual abode, or registered office in other Member States, as well as for intra-Community distance sales pursuant to Section 3c, paragraph 1, sentences 2 and 3, did not exceed €10,000 in the preceding calendar year and does not exceed this amount in the current calendar year. The supplier may declare to the tax office that it waives the application of paragraph 3. This declaration is binding on the supplier for at least two calendar years. (6) If an entrepreneur who operates his business from a location in a third country, 1. a service referred to in paragraph 3 no. 2 or the long-term rental of a means of transport, 2. a service referred to in paragraph 4 sentence 2 numbers 1 to 10 to a legal person under public law resident in Germany or 3. a service referred to in paragraph 5 sentence 2 numbers 1 and 2, Notwithstanding paragraph 1, paragraph 3 number 2, paragraph 4 sentence 1 or paragraph 5, this service is to be treated as being performed domestically if it is used or exploited there. If the service is performed by a permanent establishment of a business, sentence 1 applies accordingly if the permanent establishment is located in a third country. (7) If a business operator who runs their business from within Germany rents out a rail vehicle, a motor coach, or a road vehicle designed exclusively for the transport of goods on a short-term basis, this service shall, notwithstanding paragraph 3 no. 2, be treated as being performed in a third country if the service is provided to a business operator established in a third country, the vehicle is intended for that operator's business, and it is used in that third country. If the vehicle is rented out by a permanent establishment of a business operator, the first sentence shall apply accordingly if the permanent establishment is located within Germany. (8) If a business provides a goods transport service, loading, unloading, transshipment or similar services related to the transport of an item within the meaning of Section 3b paragraph 2, work on movable tangible property or an appraisal of such property, a travel service within the meaning of Section 25 paragraph 1 sentence 5 or an event service in connection with trade fairs and exhibitions, this service shall, notwithstanding paragraph 2, be treated as being performed in a third country if the service is used or exploited there.
